Sleepwalk Press is an independent publisher of books by artists, working across photography, collage, drawing, essay, and fiction.

We work closely with artists and writers at any stage of their careers to realise new works of cultural expression. SLEEPWALK is based in Prague and distributes internationally. Get in touch.
